## WHAT YOU'LL PARSE

### 20.14 — DRV2605L Haptic Driver
- I2C address (0x5A on **Bus 2** per v7.2)
- Register map (MODE, GO, WAVESEQ, FEEDBACK_CTRL, STATUS)
- Waveform library (click, buzz, alert; amplitude, duration)
- Drive strength control (voltage output range)
- Mode select (internal trigger, PWM, I2C)
- Current consumption (idle, active waveform, sweep)
- ERM vs. LRA (datasheet assumes ERM; confirm compatibility with ELV1411A LRA)
- Thermal limits

### 20.15 — WS2812B RGB LED (RMT Protocol)
- Protocol (1-wire timing; 0 vs. 1 bit encoding)
- Color format (GRB order? RGB order?)
- Update rate (refresh constraint)
- Power consumption (per color, per LED)
- RMT peripheral requirements (GPIO42, timing accuracy)
- Current limits (max per LED; 5V rail constraint)

### 20.16 — Crown Encoder
- Contact bounce time (typical debounce window)
- A/B quadrature phase relationship
- Maximum rotation rate (RPM → Hz conversion)
- Electrical specs (contact resistance, voltage limits)
- PCNT peripheral for debounce-free counting

### 20.17 — Flashlight LED + Gate MOSFET
**LED specs:**
- Forward voltage (3.2V typical for white)
- Maximum forward current
- Power dissipation (@rated current, de-rated with temperature)

**MOSFET (BSS138W):**
- Gate threshold voltage (Vgs(th))
- Rds(on) at 3.3V gate
- Maximum drain current (continuous)
- Thermal resistance (θja)

## 5V BOOST ARBITRATION SUMMARY

Add a section comparing all 5V consumers:
- **MAX30101 LEDs:** ~200 mA during active HR measurement
- **WS2812:** ~60 mA max (all white at full brightness)
- **Flashlight:** ~200–500 mA depending on brightness
- **BQ25619 boost capacity:** 1A total
- **Conflict:** If all three demand 5V simultaneously, boost can't supply. Firmware must sequence or limit.
