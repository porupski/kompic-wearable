## PHASE 5 GOAL

Close out the datasheet extraction with **peripherals and miscellaneous** components. These have lower firmware criticality but are essential for correctness: storage (SD), audio input (PDM mic), RF (antennas), and protection (ESD diodes, crystal specs).

By the end of Phase 5, every datasheet is parsed, all extracted specs are organized, and the firmware team has a complete hardware reference library.

## WHAT YOU'LL PARSE

### 20.18 — SD Card (SDMMC Interface)
**File:** Generic SDMMC connector spec or LCSC reference  
**Focus:**
- Electrical specs (voltage, current limits)
- Timing constraints (setup/hold for GPIO38/39/40)
- Pull-up requirements (v7.2 says 10kΩ typical)
- Card detection (CD pin polarity if used; v7.2 says "verify on bench")
- Hot-swap safe (can ESP32 handle card removal during operation?)
- Speed grades (SD High-Speed, SDXC)

### 20.19 — PDM Microphone
**File:** Generic PDM MEMS spec or LCSC reference  
**Focus:**
- I2S protocol (CLK on GPIO47, DATA on GPIO48 per v7.2)
- Clock frequency (typical 1–4 MHz)
- Sampling rates supported (16, 32, 48 kHz typical)
- Bit depth (output resolution)
- Sensitivity (dBV/Pa or similar)
- Signal-to-noise ratio (SNR)
- Frequency response (20 Hz–20 kHz)
- Power consumption (active, standby)
- Acoustic overload point (SPL limit)

### 20.20 — Miscellaneous (Reference Specs Only)
**Collated specs for:**
- **BLE antenna (Johanson 2450AT18A):** Impedance, gain, pattern, tuning network values (per v7.2)
- **GPS antenna (ceramic patch):** Frequency (L1 1.575 GHz), gain, impedance
- **RTC crystal (32.768 kHz):** Load capacitance, ESR, tolerance, temperature range
- **ESD diode arrays (ESDALCL5, TPD2E001):** Clamping voltage, leakage current, response time
- **Schottky diodes (1N4148):** Forward voltage, leakage, reverse recovery time (for GPS/RTC backup isolation)
